Straight talk about coverage

Tech, software, data, and startup roles are the deepest today, because that is where the modern job systems we read are strongest. Professional fields like finance, healthcare, legal, and education are live and growing fast as we add more sources.

We do not scrape LinkedIn or Indeed, and we never list a role we cannot read in full. We would rather show you fewer roles we can explain than a pile we cannot.

Coming next: large-enterprise systems and government roles, which will especially deepen the professional fields above. This page updates on its own as that happens.
