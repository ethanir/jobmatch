Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Jobrolu ("Jobrolu", "we", "us") collects when you use jobrolu.com, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using Jobrolu, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password. Your password is stored only as a salted hash, never in plain text.
  • Profile and resume content. If you upload a resume or build a profile, we process its contents, which may include your name, contact details, education, work history, skills, certifications, and the roles and locations you are targeting. We use this to match you to jobs and build your feed.
  • Payment information. If you upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not see or store your full card number; Stripe handles card data under its own security standards. We receive only limited details, such as whether a payment succeeded.
  • Usage and device information. Like most websites, we collect basic usage data (pages viewed, actions taken, approximate location, browser and device type) through cookies and analytics.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core service: rank job listings against your profile, build and refresh your feed, and generate application drafts.
  • To create and secure your account and keep you signed in.
  • To process your Pro purchase and provide customer support.
  • To understand how the product is used and to improve it.
  • To contact you about your account or important service changes.

Third parties we work with

We share the minimum data needed with service providers that help us run Jobrolu:

  • Anthropic processes your profile and job descriptions through its AI models to score how well each role fits you. This happens only when ranking is run.
  • Stripe processes payments for Pro.
  • Google Analytics provides aggregate usage analytics.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers store the data needed to run the service.
  • Job sources. We fetch public job listings from sources such as Adzuna, USAJOBS, and public applicant-tracking-system feeds. We pull listings from them; we do not send your resume or profile to them.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers.

Cookies

We use a sign-in cookie to keep you logged in, and analytics cookies to measure usage. You can block cookies in your browser settings, though some features, such as staying signed in, may not work without them.

How long we keep your data

We keep your account and profile data while your account is active. If you delete your account or ask us to delete your data, we remove it within a reasonable period, except where we must keep limited records (for example, payment records) to meet legal obligations.

Your choices and rights

You can view and edit your profile at any time, and you can ask us to provide a copy of your data, correct it, or delete it. Depending on where you live (for example, California and several other US states), you may have additional rights under local privacy laws. To make any request, email us at the address below and we will respond as required by applicable law.

Security

We protect your data with measures such as hashed passwords and encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Jobrolu is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their information. If you are under 16, please do not use the service.

Where your data is processed

Jobrolu is operated from the United States, and your information is processed there. If you access it from outside the US, you consent to that processing.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be communicated by email.

Contact us

Questions or privacy requests: jobrolusupport@gmail.com.
