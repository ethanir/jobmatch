Why it's different

Most job tools match keywords. This one understands.

Reads the whole job

Before ranking, it fetches the full posting and feeds it to the AI, so a role that quietly requires a security clearance or 5+ years gets caught, not mislabeled a match.

"Strong" means verified

The free filter can never award "strong." Only the AI, after reading the full description, can. Every green match is one it actually analyzed.

Built from your profile

Drop in your resume, or let your own AI describe you. JobMatch builds a structured profile, you verify it, and every ranking is judged against the real you.

Outreach, drafted

Each strong match comes with a tailored email ready to send, plus a one-click LinkedIn recruiter search. You edit and send. Never auto-spammed.

Fresh on every refresh

One click re-runs the whole pipeline. New roles appear and are flagged; everything you've seen persists. Cheap, because caching only pays for genuinely-new jobs.

Or rank for $0

Bring your own AI: export your top jobs, paste them into the free web Claude or ChatGPT, paste the result back. Same ranked feed, zero API cost.
